Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Text Launcher X (the “App”). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

2. Information We Collect

During operation, the App may collect the following information:

  • Installed apps list: To display and manage apps, the App obtains the list of applications installed on your device, including app names and package names.
  • Basic device information: The App may obtain basic device information (e.g., device model, Android version) to ensure proper functionality.

3. How We Use Information

The information collected by the App is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Display and manage the app list
  • Provide app search functionality
  • Save home screen app shortcut settings
  • Ensure the App operates properly on your device

4. Network Connectivity

The App does not connect to the internet. All features are implemented locally on your device.

5. Data Storage

The App uses SharedPreferences to store user settings, such as home screen app lists. This data is stored only on your device and is not uploaded to any server.

6. Information Sharing

The App does not share your information with any third parties and does not record user behavior.

7. Permissions

The App requires the following permissions:

  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: Allows the App to start at device boot to timely update time and date display.
  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES: Allows the App to access information about all installed apps on the device to display and manage the app list.

8. Security Measures

We implement the following measures to protect your information:

  • All data is stored locally on the device
  • No collection of personally identifiable information
  • No connection to any external servers
  • No logging of user behavior

9. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will notify you within the App.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: itoolstudio@icloud.com

11. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies only to this App and does not apply to other apps or websites that may be accessed via links within the App.
